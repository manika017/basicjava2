Number of bits and their capacity ( how many values)

number of bits | number of values  | values 
    1 (2^1)        |            2                 |   0-1        
    2 (2^2)        |            4                 |   0-3
    3 (2^3)        |            8                 |   0-7
    4 (2^4)        |           16                |   0-15
    5 (2^5)        |           32                |   0-31
    6 (2^6)        |           64                |   0-63
    7 (2^7)        |          128               |   0-127
    8 (2^8)        |          256               |   0-128

8 bits = 1 byte
1024 bytes (10^3) = 1 kilo byte
1024 x 1024 (10^6) = 1 mega byte
1024 x 1024 x 1024 bytes (10^9) = 1 giga byte
